## 📋 Summary

Version 2.0.0 is a comprehensive content release that expands the ERDA book from 9 to 14 chapters. New focal areas include AI governance, civic resilience, democratic transformation rules, strategic sovereignty, and a complete alliance architecture with a draft treaty. Real-world war insights (Zaluzhnyi/Chatham House 2026) are integrated across chapters.

---

## ✨ Changes

### New chapters

- **Chapter 10 – The AI Concept:** European AI governance framework with a Co-evolution Index (KEI), ethical guardrails and defence-AI regulation. Extended with Defence & Security (swarm-AI governance, autonomous darkness).
- **Chapter 11 – The Citizen Concept (expanded):** Integrated civic duty model (civil/social/ecological/military), demographic imperative and technological mobilisation. 4 sub-chapters.
- **Chapter 12 – The Democracy Concept:** Seven transformation rules of social democracy. Role-play as a quality-assurance method.
- **Chapter 13 – Strategic Sovereignty:** 7 sub-chapters on self-deterrence, air defence, industrial mass, financial unblocking, technological sovereignty, hybrid defence and parallel strategy.
- **Chapter 14 – The Democratic Coalitions of the Willing:** Constitutional sketch (Art. 1–30), ten sectoral coalitions, club architecture EDDRC, tier model (Tier 0–9), democracy chain, six treaty annexes (Annexes 14.A–14.F) with 155 articles.

### Revisions to existing chapters

- **EDA (Ch. 5):** 5.9.8 Operational validation of drone/robotic warfare (swarm autonomy, counter-swarm, Ukraine as an innovation partner).
- **FORTERA (Ch. 7):** 7.3.5.1 Immediate programme "1,000 days" extended with attrition production.

### Appendices & cross-cutting

- **D.5 Strategy paper:** Europe resilient against Russia's war logic ("negotiation as a weapon").
- **D.6 Block D:** Executive summary for Chapters 10–14.
- **Appendix I (Glossary):** 11 new terms for Chapters 10–14 (KEI, co-evolution, citizen duty model, self-deterrence, EDDRC, democracy chain, etc.).
- **Zaluzhnyi integration:** Insights on kill zones, transparent battlespace, attrition weapons and the demographic imperative across 6 chapters (5, 7, 10, 11, 13).
