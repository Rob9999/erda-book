# 5.10.2 Immediate measures (2025–2030)

_**Immediate measures (2025–2030)**_

* Strategic **stationing agreements with France**, e.g. along the eastern border of the EDA (Poland, Finland, Baltic states).
* Establishment of a **Nuclear Consultation Council (NCC)** within the EDA, modelled on NATO’s Nuclear Planning Group – for transparency and multilateral coordination.
